Cheesecake

Description

Rich and creamy cheesecake is one of those desserts that always feels like an extra special indulgence. And with these easy tips, you can make cheesecake at home that rivals anything you can order in a restaurant.

But even the best cheesecake recipes don't always tell you everything you need to know, so we're filling in the blanks with tips and techniques to help you avoid lumps, leaks, cracks, and sunken middles. Here's how to make cheesecake, from the crust to the filling, and from baking to cooling.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust:

  • 1 cup graham cracker crumbs
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar

Cheesecake:

  • 4 (8 ounce) packages full-fat cream cheese, at room temperature
  • 1 ½ cups white sugar
  • 1 cup full-fat sour cream, at room temperature
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs, at room temperature

For Serving:

  • 1 (21 ounce) can cherry pie filling (Optional)
